Covid Oriented Food Delivery

The Pandemic has taken a toll on all of us. While some of us are safe and sound, others are still fighting this deadly virus. Being bedridden and with the weakness associated with the illness can be a real challenging task to prepare food. We strive to overcome this difficulty by creating a network between NGOs and independent donors who wish to donate food for the presently infected, self-quarantined patients.

Supply of Medicines

Going outdoor to buy medicines, waiting for your turn at the pharmacy, sacrificing your ideal social distancing policies can be a tedious task for many, especially not knowing whether or not the pharmacy has the medicine you require. We collaborated with some of the pharmacies, updated their databases, and made it easy for anyone to check and order their medications.

Consultation

Consulting an expert is always a better option than trusting other online resources. We contacted some of the doctors and requested them to allocate some time for online consultation. We created a database of doctors, sorting them according to their specializations and mentioning their consultation hours.
